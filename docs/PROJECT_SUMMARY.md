ComplianceIQ is a 6-agent EU AI Act compliance intelligence system built on Microsoft Foundry and Foundry IQ. 

PROBLEM: EU AI Act full enforcement in 2026 means every organization using AI for EU customers faces mandatory compliance. Manual audits cost $50,000-$200,000 and take 6-8 weeks. Small companies cannot afford this.

SOLUTION: ComplianceIQ automates the process: upload AI system documentation, get a full compliance audit in under 3 minutes.

AGENTS: Scanner extracts system characteristics. Gap Analyzer uses Foundry IQ to check 10 EU AI Act requirements with exact article citations. Risk Scorer applies Annex III deterministic classification. Remediation Planner queries Foundry IQ for prioritized action steps. Report Generator writes board-level, technical, and formal reports. Verification Agent cross-checks every claim against Foundry IQ sources.

IQ INTEGRATION: Foundry IQ knowledge base indexes EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, and live enforcement guidance. Agentic retrieval mode plans queries, searches in parallel, and returns cited, grounded answers — exactly the kind of permission-aware, hallucination-resistant retrieval that regulatory compliance demands.

IMPACT: Democratizes compliance. Any organization can run a professional EU AI Act audit for free.
